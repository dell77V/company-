About Us

Discover the story behind Zambia's most innovative technology and creative solutions company

Pioneering Digital Innovation in Zambia

Founded in 2025 by visionaries John Pupwe and Elizabeth Nfungulwe, Joazen Enterprise emerged from a shared passion to position Zambia as a leader in technology and creative industries.

Our Mission

To deliver innovative digital solutions that empower Zambian businesses while preserving and promoting our cultural heritage through creative expression.

Our Vision

To be Zambia's premier technology and creative hub, recognized globally for excellence in digital innovation and African-inspired design.

Our Values

Innovation, Integrity, Excellence, Cultural Pride, and Community Development guide everything we do at Joazen Enterprise.

Meet Our Founders

Our Founders

The visionary leaders behind Joazen Enterprise's success story

John Pupwe

Co-Founder & Chief Executive Officer

Technology visionary with over a decade of experience in software development and digital transformation. John holds a Master's degree in Computer Science from the University of Zambia and previously led digital innovation at one of Zambia's largest financial institutions.

Elizabeth Nfungulwe

Co-Founder & Creative Director

Award-winning designer with expertise in both digital interfaces and fashion. Elizabeth holds degrees in both Computer Science and Fashion Design, bringing a unique blend of technical and creative skills to her leadership role. She has been recognized by Forbes Africa as one of Zambia's most influential young entrepreneurs.
